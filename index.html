Installation guide

How to install a section

From browsing to live on your store — step by step.

Step 1 — Install SectionDrop

Go to the Shopify App Store and install SectionDrop. Free to install. A short onboarding takes about one minute.

SectionDrop requires a Shopify 2.0 theme. Vintage/legacy themes are not supported.

Step 2 — Browse the library

Open the Library in the app. Filter by category (Forms, Content, Conversion) or by market (International, Nederland). Click any section for the full description and preview image.

Step 3 — Try before you buy

Click "Try section" on the detail page. SectionDrop creates a private copy of your active theme and installs the section there — 20–40 seconds.

Your live store is never affected. The preview theme is private and unpublished. It cannot be published by anyone.

When ready, click "Open preview editor" to see the section in the Shopify theme editor with your own theme's design.

Step 4 — Purchase

Click "Buy for €9". Redirected to Shopify's payment flow. Returns to SectionDrop automatically after approval.

All payments go through Shopify billing and appear in your Shopify billing overview.

Step 5 — Install in your theme

On the install page, select your theme and click Install.

1
Select your themeChoose any installed theme — live, development, or backup.
2
Click InstallThe section's Liquid file is written directly to your theme.

Open the theme editorAdmin → Online Store → Themes → Customize. The section is available under "Add section".

Step 6 — Customize

In the theme editor, click "Add section", search for the section name, add it to any page. Customize everything — colors, fonts, spacing, content — no code required.

Sections automatically match your theme's colors and typography.

Reinstalling in a different theme

My Sections → find the section → Install → select a different theme. No limit on number of themes.

Applying updates

When an update is available, a badge appears in My Sections. Click "Update & reinstall". Always free for purchased sections.

Getting started
Does SectionDrop work with my theme?
SectionDrop works with all Shopify 2.0 themes — Dawn, Horizon, Refresh, Craft, Sense and any other 2.0 theme. Sections automatically use your theme's fonts and colors. Vintage/legacy themes (pre-2.0) are not supported.

Try Before You Buy
How does Try Before You Buy work?
Click "Try section". SectionDrop creates a private copy of your active theme and installs the section in it — 20–40 seconds. Preview and customize freely. Your live store is never affected.
Will my live store be affected during preview?
No. The preview creates a private, unpublished copy of your theme. Your live store is completely unchanged.
Why does the preview take 20–40 seconds?
Creating a preview duplicates your entire theme — all files, assets and settings. We can't speed this up; it depends on your theme size and Shopify's processing time.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 22, 2026

1. Who we are

SectionDrop is developed and operated by Ecom Studio Nimma. Questions: hallo@ecomstudionimma.nl.

2. What data we collect

When you install and use SectionDrop, we collect:

  • Your Shopify store domain and access token
  • Purchase records (which sections you have purchased)
  • Installation records (which sections are installed in which themes)
  • Reviews submitted in the app
  • Section ideas via the Suggest feature
  • Your merchant name (from onboarding)

We do not collect data from your store's customers.

3. How we use your data

Solely to operate the app — authentication, purchases, section installation and update notifications. We do not sell your data.

4. Data storage

Stored in a secure database on Vercel infrastructure. Access tokens used exclusively to communicate with Shopify's API on your behalf.

5. Retention and deletion

Session data removed on uninstall. Full deletion: email hallo@ecomstudionimma.nl. We comply with Shopify's GDPR webhooks.

6. Third-party services

  • Shopify — authentication, billing and theme API
  • Vercel — hosting and infrastructure
  • Anthropic Claude API — Store Analyzer (HTML analyzed, not stored)

7. Your rights

Right to access, correct or delete your data. Email hallo@ecomstudionimma.nl.

8. Changes

We may update this policy. The date at the top reflects the most recent update.
