Installation guide

How to install a section

From browsing to live on your store — this guide walks you through every step.

Step 1 — Install the SectionDrop app

Go to the Shopify App Store and install SectionDrop. The app is free to install. After installing, you will be guided through a short onboarding that takes about one minute.

SectionDrop works with any Shopify 2.0 theme. If you are using a vintage theme (pre-2.0), sections cannot be installed.

Step 2 — Browse the section library

Open the Library in the app. You can filter sections by category (Forms, Content, Conversion) and by market (International, Nederland). Each section has a description and a preview image.

Step 3 — Try before you buy (optional)

Click "Try section" on any section detail page. SectionDrop will create a private copy of your active theme and install the section there. This takes about 20–40 seconds.

!
Your live store is never affected during the preview. The preview theme is a private, unpublished copy. It cannot be published by anyone.

Once the preview is ready, click "Open preview editor" to open the Shopify theme editor with the section already installed. Add the section to any page to see how it looks and works in your own theme.

Step 4 — Purchase the section

When you are ready to keep a section, click "Buy for €9" on the section detail page. You will be redirected to Shopify's payment flow. After payment is approved, you are redirected back to SectionDrop.

All payments go through Shopify's billing system. You will see the charge in your Shopify billing overview.

Step 5 — Install in your theme

After purchase, you are taken to the install page. Select the theme you want to install the section in and click "Install". The section is added to your theme immediately.

1
Select theme Choose any of your installed Shopify themes from the dropdown.
2
Click Install The section file is written directly to your theme. No code changes needed.
3
Open the theme editor Go to your Shopify admin → Online Store → Themes → Customize. The section is now available under "Add section".

Step 6 — Customize in the theme editor

Click "Add section" in the theme editor sidebar and search for the section name. Add it to any page. All settings — colors, fonts, spacing, labels — are adjustable directly in the editor. No code required.

Sections automatically inherit your theme's color scheme and typography. You don't need to match fonts or colors manually.

Reinstalling in a different theme

Purchased sections can be reinstalled in any theme at any time. Go to My Sections in the app, find the section, click "Install" and select a different theme. There is no limit on the number of themes.

Receiving updates

When a purchased section receives an update, a badge appears in My Sections. Click "Update & reinstall" to apply the update to your chosen theme. Your existing content and settings in the theme editor are preserved as long as no setting IDs were changed.

Getting started
Does SectionDrop work with my theme?
SectionDrop works with all Shopify 2.0 themes, including Dawn, Horizon, Refresh, Craft, Sense, and any other theme built on the 2.0 architecture. Sections automatically inherit your theme's fonts, colors and color schemes. If you are using a vintage/legacy theme (pre-2.0), sections cannot be installed. Not sure if your theme is 2.0? Check in your Shopify admin under Online Store → Themes. If you see the "Customize" button and sections in the editor sidebar, you are on a 2.0 theme.
Do I need to know how to code?
No. Sections are installed with one click from the app. Everything is customizable through the Shopify theme editor — colors, typography, spacing, labels and content. No code changes are required at any point.
How do I install SectionDrop?
Go to the Shopify App Store, search for SectionDrop and click Install. After installation, you will be guided through a short onboarding. The app is free to install — you only pay when you purchase individual sections.
Are sections available in languages other than English?
Most sections are available in English and work in any language — all text labels are fully customizable in the theme editor. Some sections are also available specifically for the Dutch market, with Dutch default text. You can filter the library by market. More language-specific sections are being added regularly.
Purchasing & ownership
How much does a section cost?
Each section costs €9, one-time. There are no recurring fees for individual sections. The app also offers a Pro plan at €16.99/month or €169.90/year for unlimited access to all sections.
What does "one-time purchase" mean exactly?
You pay once and the section is yours permanently. When you install a section, the Liquid code is written directly into your theme. You own that code — it does not depend on SectionDrop remaining active. Even if you cancel the app, your installed sections continue to work.
Can I reinstall a section in a different theme?
Yes. Purchased sections can be reinstalled in any of your themes, as many times as you need, at no extra cost. Go to My Sections in the app, select the section and choose the theme you want to install it in.
Is there a limit on how many themes I can install a section in?
No. Once you purchase a section, you can install it in as many themes as you want — development themes, backup themes, a completely different live theme. There is no limit.
Are updates included?
Yes. When a section you purchased receives an update, you will see an "Update available" badge in My Sections. Click "Update & reinstall" to apply the update. Updates are always free for sections you have purchased.
Try Before You Buy
How does Try Before You Buy work?
When you click "Try section", SectionDrop creates a private copy of your active theme and installs the section in that copy. This takes about 20–40 seconds. You then get a link to the Shopify theme editor for that private theme, where you can add the section to any page and customize it freely. Your live store is never affected.
Will my live store be affected during the preview?
No. The preview always uses a private, unpublished copy of your theme. It cannot be published. Your live store remains completely unchanged. Nothing is installed in your actual theme until you purchase and choose to install.
Why does the preview take 20–40 seconds?
Creating the preview requires duplicating your entire theme, which involves copying all template files, assets and settings. Shopify processes this in the background and we wait for it to complete before installing the section. We cannot speed this up — it depends on the size of your theme and Shopify's processing time.
Pro plan
What is included in Pro?
SectionDrop Pro gives you unlimited access to all current and future sections. Instead of paying €9 per section, you can install any section directly. New sections added to the library are automatically included. Pro is available monthly at €16.99/month or yearly at €169.90/year (equivalent to 2 months free).
What happens to my installed sections if I cancel Pro?
Nothing happens to sections already installed in your theme. They continue to work exactly as before. Cancelling Pro means you lose the ability to install new sections without purchasing them individually. Your installed sections stay in your theme indefinitely.
Can I switch between monthly and yearly Pro?
Yes. You can switch plans at any time from the Pro page in the app. The switch takes effect at the start of your next billing period.
Support
How do I get support?
Email us at hallo@ecomstudionimma.nl. We respond within 1 business day. For quick questions, you can also use the contact options inside the app.
A section is not displaying correctly in my theme. What should I do?
First, check that your theme is a Shopify 2.0 theme. Second, make sure the section has been installed (go to My Sections and reinstall if needed). Third, check in the theme editor that the section is added to your page template. If the issue persists, send us an email with your store URL and the section name — we will investigate within 1 business day.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 22, 2026

1. Who we are

SectionDrop is a Shopify app developed and operated by Ecom Studio Nimma. If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at hallo@ecomstudionimma.nl.

2. What data we collect

When you install and use SectionDrop, we collect:

  • Your Shopify store domain and access token (required for authentication and API access)
  • Purchase records (which sections you have purchased)
  • Installation records (which sections are installed in which themes)
  • Reviews you submit within the app
  • Section ideas you submit via the Suggest feature
  • Your merchant name (entered during onboarding)

We do not collect data from your store's customers.

3. How we use your data

We use your data solely to operate the SectionDrop app:

  • Authenticating your session
  • Processing purchases and managing your section library
  • Installing and updating sections in your Shopify theme
  • Sending update notifications for sections you have purchased

We do not sell your data to third parties.

4. Data storage

Your data is stored in a secure database on Vercel infrastructure. Access tokens are stored as required by Shopify's OAuth flow and used exclusively to communicate with Shopify's API on your behalf.

5. Data retention and deletion

When you uninstall SectionDrop, your session data is removed automatically. You can request full deletion of all your data by contacting us at hallo@ecomstudionimma.nl. We comply with Shopify's mandatory GDPR webhooks for data erasure.

6. Third-party services

  • Shopify — authentication, billing and theme API access
  • Vercel — hosting and infrastructure
  • Anthropic Claude API — Store Analyzer feature (page HTML is analyzed but not stored)

7. Your rights

You have the right to access, correct or delete your personal data. Contact us at hallo@ecomstudionimma.nl.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top reflects the most recent update.
