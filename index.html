Does SectionDrop work with my theme?
SectionDrop works with all Shopify 2.0 themes — Dawn, Horizon, Refresh, Craft, Sense, and any other 2.0 theme. Sections automatically inherit your theme's fonts and colors. Vintage/legacy themes (pre-2.0) are not supported.
Do I need to know how to code?
No. Sections are installed with one click. All customization happens in the Shopify theme editor — colors, fonts, spacing, labels and content. No code at any point.
What does "one-time purchase" mean exactly?
You pay once and the Liquid code is written directly into your theme. You own that code. It works independently of SectionDrop — even if you uninstall the app, your sections keep working.
Can I install a section in multiple themes?
Yes. Once purchased, you can reinstall any section in as many themes as you want — development themes, backup themes, a new live theme. No limit, no extra cost.

Are updates free?

Installation guide

How to install a section

From browsing to live on your store — step by step.

Step 1 — Install SectionDrop

Go to the Shopify App Store and install SectionDrop. The app is free to install. After installing, a short onboarding guides you through the basics — about one minute.

SectionDrop requires a Shopify 2.0 theme. Vintage/legacy themes are not supported.

Step 2 — Browse the library

Open the Library in the app. Filter by category (Forms, Content, Conversion) or by market (International, Nederland). Click any section to see the full description and preview image.

Step 3 — Try before you buy

On the section detail page, click "Try section". SectionDrop creates a private copy of your active theme and installs the section there. This takes 20–40 seconds.

Your live store is never affected. The preview theme is a private, unpublished copy that cannot be published by anyone.

When the preview is ready, click "Open preview editor". The Shopify theme editor opens with the section already installed. Add it to any page and customize it to see exactly how it looks.

Step 4 — Purchase

Click "Buy for €9" on the section detail page. You are redirected to Shopify's payment flow. After approval, you return to SectionDrop automatically.

All payments go through Shopify's billing system and appear in your Shopify billing overview.

Step 5 — Install in your theme

After purchase you land on the install page. Select the theme you want to install the section in and click "Install". Done.

1
Select your themeChoose any installed theme from the dropdown — your live theme, a development copy, whatever you need.
2
Click InstallThe section's Liquid file is written directly to your theme. No code changes needed on your end.
3
Open the theme editorShopify admin → Online Store → Themes → Customize. The section is now available under "Add section".

Step 6 — Customize

In the theme editor sidebar, click "Add section" and search for the section name. Add it to any page. All settings — colors, fonts, spacing, labels, content — are adjustable in the editor. No code required.

Sections automatically match your theme's color scheme and typography. No manual matching needed.

Reinstalling in a different theme

Go to My Sections → find the section → click "Install" → select a different theme. You can do this as many times as you need. There is no limit on the number of themes.

Applying updates

When a section receives an update, a badge appears in My Sections. Click "Update & reinstall" to apply it to your chosen theme. Updates are always free.

Try Before You Buy
How does Try Before You Buy work?
Click "Try section" on any section. SectionDrop creates a private copy of your active theme and installs the section in it. This takes 20–40 seconds. You get a link to the Shopify theme editor for that private theme where you can preview and customize freely. Your live store is never affected.
Will my live store be affected during the preview?
No. The preview uses a private, unpublished copy of your theme. Your live store is completely unchanged. Nothing is installed in your actual theme until you purchase and explicitly choose to install.
Why does the preview take 20–40 seconds?
Creating a preview requires duplicating your entire theme — all template files, assets and settings. Shopify processes this in the background and we wait for it to complete. We cannot speed this up — it depends on your theme's size and Shopify's processing time.

Pro gives you unlimited access to all current and future sections. Instead of paying €9 per section, you install any section directly. New sections are included automatically. Available at €16.99/month or €169.90/year (2 months free).

Nothing. Sections already installed continue working exactly as before. Cancelling Pro means you can no longer install new sections without purchasing them individually.
Can I switch between monthly and yearly?
Yes, from the Pro page in the app at any time. The change takes effect at the start of your next billing period.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 22, 2026

1. Who we are

SectionDrop is a Shopify app developed and operated by Ecom Studio Nimma. Questions? Contact us at hallo@ecomstudionimma.nl.

2. What data we collect

When you install and use SectionDrop, we collect:

  • Your Shopify store domain and access token (required for authentication and API access)
  • Purchase records (which sections you have purchased)
  • Installation records (which sections are installed in which themes)
  • Reviews you submit within the app
  • Section ideas submitted via the Suggest feature
  • Your merchant name (entered during onboarding)

We do not collect data from your store's customers.

3. How we use your data

We use your data solely to operate the SectionDrop app — authenticating your session, processing purchases, installing and updating sections, and sending update notifications. We do not sell your data.

4. Data storage

Your data is stored in a secure database on Vercel infrastructure. Access tokens are stored as required by Shopify's OAuth flow and used exclusively to communicate with Shopify's API on your behalf.

5. Data retention and deletion

When you uninstall SectionDrop, your session data is removed automatically. For full deletion of all data, email hallo@ecomstudionimma.nl. We comply with Shopify's mandatory GDPR webhooks.

6. Third-party services

  • Shopify — authentication, billing and theme API access
  • Vercel — hosting and infrastructure
  • Anthropic Claude API — Store Analyzer feature (page HTML is analyzed, not stored)

7. Your rights

You have the right to access, correct or delete your personal data. Contact us at hallo@ecomstudionimma.nl.

8. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top reflects the most recent update.
