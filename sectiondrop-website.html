FAQ

Common questions.

Does SectionDrop work with my theme?
SectionDrop works with all Shopify 2.0 themes, including Dawn, Horizon, Refresh, and any other theme built on the 2.0 architecture. Sections automatically inherit your theme's fonts and colors.
Do I need to know how to code?
No. Sections are installed directly from the app with one click. Everything is customizable through the Shopify theme editor — no code required.
What happens to my sections if I cancel Pro?
Sections that are already installed in your theme remain there. Cancelling Pro only means you lose access to install new sections without purchasing them individually.
Can I try a section before buying?
Yes. Every section can be previewed in a private copy of your own theme before you purchase. Your live store is never affected during the preview.
Can I reinstall a section in a different theme?
Yes. Purchased sections can be reinstalled in any of your themes at any time, as many times as you need.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 22, 2026

1. Who we are

SectionDrop is a Shopify app developed and operated by Ecom Studio Nimma. If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can contact us at hallo@ecomstudionimma.nl.

2. What data we collect

When you install and use SectionDrop, we collect the following data:

  • Your Shopify store domain and access token (required for authentication and API access)
  • Purchase records (which sections you have purchased)
  • Installation records (which sections are installed in which themes)
  • Reviews you submit within the app
  • Section ideas you submit via the Suggest feature
  • Your merchant name (entered during onboarding)

We do not collect data from your store's customers.

3. How we use your data

We use your data solely to operate the SectionDrop app. This includes:

  • Authenticating your session
  • Processing purchases and managing your section library
  • Installing and updating sections in your Shopify theme
  • Sending update notifications for sections you have purchased

We do not sell your data to third parties.

4. Data storage

Your data is stored in a secure database hosted on Vercel infrastructure. Access tokens are stored as required by Shopify's OAuth flow and are used exclusively to communicate with Shopify's API on your behalf.

5. Data retention and deletion

When you uninstall SectionDrop, your session data is removed automatically. You can request full deletion of all your data by contacting us at hallo@ecomstudionimma.nl. We comply with Shopify's mandatory GDPR webhooks for data erasure.

6. Third-party services

SectionDrop uses the following third-party services:

  • Shopify — for authentication, billing, and theme API access
  • Vercel — for hosting and infrastructure
  • Anthropic Claude API — for the Store Analyzer feature (page HTML is analyzed but not stored)

7. Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us at hallo@ecomstudionimma.nl.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent update.
